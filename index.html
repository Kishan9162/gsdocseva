Frequently Asked Questions

Do you store my photos?
+
Absolutely not! Your photos are processed entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to our servers. All resizing happens on your device, so your images remain completely private and secure.
Which exams are supported?
+
We support SSC (CGL, CHSL, MTS), UPSC Civil Services, Railway RRB NTPC, IBPS PO/Clerk, SBI PO/Clerk, State PSC (BPSC, UPPSC), Police exams, NDA, CDS, CAPF, and many other government exams. Each has specific photo size and KB requirements which we handle automatically.
What image formats are supported?
+
We support JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP, WEBP and other common image formats. You can upload any format and download as JPG or PNG. Most government exams accept JPG format, so we recommend JPG for best compatibility.
Is the service really free?
+
Yes, completely free! GS DocSeva is a free service with no hidden charges, registration requirements, or premium versions. We believe every exam aspirant should have access to quality tools. We don't charge now and never will.
How accurate are the exam specifications?
+
Our specifications are based on official government exam requirements. However, exam rules can change. We recommend verifying the final photo specifications with the official exam authority website before submitting your form. We're constantly updating our database.
Can I resize multiple photos at once?
+
Yes! In the Exam Photo Prep tool, you can upload multiple documents (photo, signature, thumb impression, marksheet) and get all resized versions at once. Our batch processing makes it super fast.
Will resized photos be accepted by exam authorities?
+
Our tool resizes photos to official specifications. However, we cannot guarantee acceptance as standards may vary between different exams and regions. Always verify specifications with the official exam portal. If your resized photo doesn't match requirements, contact the exam authority directly.
Do I need to install anything?
+
No! GS DocSeva is a web-based tool. Just visit our website and start using it. Works on any device with a modern web browser - PC, Mac, Linux, Android, or iPhone.
What about background removal quality?
+
Our background removal uses AI-powered technology for accurate edge detection. It works best with clear, well-lit photos. You can choose different background colors or keep it transparent. For best results, ensure your photo has good contrast with the background.
